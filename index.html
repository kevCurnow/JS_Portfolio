About Me


My coding journey truly began when I decided to dive head-first into the industry by attending a 12 week intensive .NET bootcamp at Eleven Fifty Academy. I enjoyed the few programming classes I took in high school and college, but didn't consider pursuing it as a career at the time. At Eleven Fifty I developed new technical skills utilizing languages such as C# and Angular, met some amazing people, and ultimately learned I was exactly where I needed to be.

After some time spent helping Eleven Fifty develop curriculum, I started my career as a software engineer at technical consulting firm Applied Information Sciences. My first year with the company was spent at GEICO, assisting in the initial rewrite of their Commercial Auto Application. Following that assignment, I spent another year on various government projects in which I acquired some experience using Microsoft Azure.

After being laid off due to COVID-19, I decided to enroll in another class at Eleven Fifty. I greatly enjoyed my time in the Web Development cohort learning new skills like JavaScript, React, Node, PostgreSql, and more while also improving my abilities with HTML and CSS and meeting more great people.

Outside of coding, I'm a man of many interests. Family is very important to me, and I love that a lot of my relatives live close by. I've also taken up playing tennis again after a decade long hiatus. In addition, you can probably find me cheering on my favorite sports teams, watching movies or random YouTube videos, or listening to a podcast while I work out. Really looking forward to being able to travel again!
